Why this exists

Obedience over views. But the tools shouldn't be the obstacle.

You've felt it for years — in prayer, in the Word, watching the world go dark and knowing you have something to say. What stopped you was never the message. It was everything around the message: what to post, how to script it, how to film it, how to edit it, when to publish it.

We know, because we did it the long way — building Deep Bible Stories one faithful upload at a time, until 80 million people had watched. Lampstand removes everything except the part only you can do — saying it, on camera, in your own voice. No AI avatars. No synthetic preaching. A lamp doesn't replace the flame; it carries it.

Honest answers

The questions you’re right to ask.

Is AI writing my message for me? Is that even authentic?

Lampstand drafts the structure — hooks, pacing, beats — from the calling you declared and the Scripture you point it to. You edit every word, and only your face and your voice ever appear on camera. No AI avatars, no synthetic preaching, ever. Think of it as a producer, not a preacher: it carries the lamp, you are the flame.

What theology does it assume?

None on its own. Lampstand writes from your declared niche and the passages you bring — it structures, you shepherd. Your convictions stay yours; the tool’s job is to keep your channel faithful to the calling you wrote in step one, not to add doctrine to it.

Do I need to be a pastor, a speaker, or good on camera?

No. You need a phone, a window with light, and about sixty seconds of saying something you actually believe. The onboarding walks you to your first published Short, and the coaching assumes you’ve never done this before — because most of the first circle hasn’t.

How much time does this take each day?

The script arrives in minutes. Recording is three takes of a sixty-second Short. As the editing and publishing stages ship, the goal is a finished, posted Short in under fifteen minutes a day — the sixty seconds on camera being the only part we’ll never automate.

Is it only for YouTube Shorts?

Shorts first — it’s the format that carried Deep Bible Stories to 80 million viewers and the one the tool is built around. The vertical Shorts you make travel naturally to Reels and TikTok, and direct publishing to those platforms is on the horizon after the YouTube loop is complete.

What does it cost?

Joining the waitlist is free, with no card and no commitment. We’ll share pricing openly with the first circle before anything launches — no surprises, and early members will get the best terms we ever offer.
